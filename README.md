**SEEDOINO** is a fully air-gapped hardware tool for securely managing your cryptocurrency **seed phrase** using a microSD card. It is designed to maximize privacy and eliminate digital exposure by operating completely offline‚Äî **no Wi-Fi**, **no Bluetooth**, no wireless interfaces at all.

## üîê Key Features

- **Seed Phrase Storage**  
  Securely store your BIP39-compatible seed phrase on a microSD card in a fully offline environment.

- **Seed Phrase Recovery**  
  Read the seed phrase directly from the SD card.

- **Seed Phrase Cloning**  
  Duplicate your seed phrase to multiple SD cards without manually re-entering it‚Äîideal for creating several backups quickly.

- **Seed Phrase Splitting**  
  Optionally split your seed phrase across two separate SD cards for enhanced redundancy and physical separation.

- **100% Air-Gapped**  
  SEEDOINO contains **no wireless interfaces**: no Wi-Fi, no Bluetooth.
